## 📖 Overview

**LoadLink** is a full-stack logistics marketplace designed to reduce unused truck cargo space by connecting truck drivers with small businesses that need affordable freight transportation.

Every day, thousands of trucks travel with partially empty cargo space, while small businesses struggle with expensive shipping options. LoadLink aims to bridge this gap by providing a platform where:

* **Truck drivers** can publish their travel routes along with available cargo capacity.
* **Business owners** can post shipment requests with destination and cargo details.
* The platform intelligently recommends suitable driver-shipment matches based on destination and available truck capacity.

The goal is to improve logistics efficiency, reduce transportation costs, and maximize truck space utilization.

## 🚀 Planned Workflow

1. Driver registers and logs into the platform.
2. Driver publishes a transport route with available cargo space.
3. Business owner posts a shipment request.
4. The backend evaluates available routes based on destination and cargo capacity.
5. The platform recommends the most suitable drivers for the shipment.
